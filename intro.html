Introduction

Welcome to GreenPlate: A Sustainable Food Management System! This semester-long project is aimed at reducing food waste and encouraging sustainable eating habits. The mobile application generates personalized calorie recommendations for users. This multifaceted food tracking app goes beyond just calorie recommendations. Users can minimize waste by customizing shopping lists, managing ingredients, and creating recipes. Join us in simplifying food preparation and encouraging healthier eating habits!
